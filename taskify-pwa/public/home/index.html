Plan tasks and stay synced with your calendar.

Taskify is a privacy-first task and planning app for managing tasks, due dates, reminders, boards, and calendar views. This page is public and does not require login so visitors can review what the app does.

Taskify also offers an optional Google Calendar integration so users can connect their Google account, view selected calendars inside Taskify, and keep upcoming events visible alongside tasks.

What Taskify Does

One place for tasks, schedules, and reminders.

Taskify helps users capture tasks, organize them into boards, add due dates and reminders, and review work in an upcoming calendar-style view.

  • Create, edit, and complete personal tasks.
  • Organize tasks by boards, lists, and upcoming dates.
  • Use reminders and calendar views to track what is next.
  • Optionally connect Google Calendar to bring calendar events into Taskify.
Google Calendar

Why Taskify requests Google access.

The Google Calendar connection is used only when a user chooses to enable it. Taskify uses that access to read the user’s selected calendars and event data so calendar activity can appear inside the app.

This integration supports Taskify’s scheduling experience and helps users review tasks and calendar events in one place.
