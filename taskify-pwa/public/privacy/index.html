Taskify Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Taskify (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides task and calendar features, including optional Google Calendar integration. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information we collect

  • Account/identity data: identifiers needed to operate Taskify (for example, app-specific public keys).
  • Task and calendar data: tasks, events, and settings you create or sync.
  • Google account data (optional): if you connect Google Calendar, we access your Google email address and read calendar/event data based on granted scopes.
  • Technical data: basic logs required for reliability, debugging, and security.

2) How we use information

  • Provide and maintain Taskify features.
  • Sync connected calendar data into your Taskify Upcoming view.
  • Improve reliability, security, and performance.
  • Respond to support or abuse reports.

3) Google API data use

If you connect Google Calendar, Taskify only requests scopes required for the feature (for example, calendar.readonly and userinfo.email).

  • Google data is used only to provide calendar integration functionality.
  • We do not sell Google user data.
  • We do not use Google Workspace APIs to develop, improve, or train generalized AI/ML models.

4) Data retention and deletion

We retain data as needed to operate Taskify and comply with legal obligations. You may disconnect Google Calendar at any time, which stops future sync and removes active connection state for that integration.

5) Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption for sensitive credentials at rest and in transit.

6) Third-party services

Taskify may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (for example, hosting and OAuth providers) to deliver service functionality.

7) Children’s privacy

Taskify is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

9) Contact
